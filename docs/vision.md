# Product vision

SurfThroughStocks will become an AI-powered market research assistant that brings technical analysis, reliable market context, news sentiment, portfolio management, and premium experiences into one deliberate workspace.

The foundation prioritizes trustworthy boundaries over feature speed. Future capabilities must be explainable, observable, secure, and independently testable. The application is designed to grow from free-tier deployment services to a platform serving hundreds of thousands of users.

## Non-goals for the foundation

- No market-data providers or synthetic market data.
- No AI provider keys, prompts, agents, or model calls.
- No investment recommendations, trading workflow, portfolio calculations, or subscriptions.
- No production authentication provider until a product decision is made.
